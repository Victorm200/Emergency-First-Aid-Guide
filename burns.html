🔥 Burns & Scalds

Burn injuries require immediate cooling and protection from infection.

📍 Types of Burns
  • First-degree: red, painful skin
  • Second-degree: blisters and swelling
  • Third-degree: charred or white skin, numbness
🩹 First Aid for Burns
  1. Cool burn under running water for at least 10 minutes
  2. Remove tight clothing or jewelry
  3. Cover with sterile, non-fluffy dressing
⚠ Avoid
  • Using ice directly on burns
  • Breaking blisters
  • Applying butter, toothpaste, or oils
