Burns & Scalds

Quick steps to treat burns before medical help arrives.

Types of Burns

First-Degree Burns
Red, minor swelling, and pain affecting only the top layer of skin.
Second-Degree Burns
Blisters, deeper skin damage, severe pain, and swelling.
Third-Degree Burns
White, charred skin; damage goes through all skin layers.
