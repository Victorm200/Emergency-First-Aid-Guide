🚑 CPR (Cardiopulmonary Resuscitation)

CPR is a life-saving technique used when someone's breathing or heartbeat has stopped. It keeps oxygenated blood flowing to vital organs until professional help arrives.

📍 When to Perform CPR
  • Person is unresponsive and not breathing normally
  • No pulse detected
  • After drowning, cardiac arrest, or choking incidents
🧍 Adult CPR Steps
  1. Call emergency services immediately (999 / 112 / 1199)
  2. Place the heel of your hand in the center of the chest
  3. Put your other hand on top and interlock fingers
  4. Push hard and fast (about 100–120 compressions per minute)
  5. After 30 compressions, give 2 rescue breaths if trained
👶 Child & Infant CPR
  • Use two fingers for infants, one hand for children
  • Compression depth: ~4cm for infants, ~5cm for children
  • Ratio: 30 compressions to 2 breaths
