Why First Aid Matters

Knowing basic first aid can save lives. Learn the simple steps you can take before professional help arrives.

CPR (Cardiopulmonary Resuscitation)

Push hard and fast in the center of the chest. 100-120 compressions per minute.

Burns

Cool the burn under running water for at least 10 minutes. Do not apply ice or butter.
