🩸 Bleeding & Wound Care

Immediate bleeding control saves lives. Follow these steps to stop bleeding and prevent infection.

📍 Types of Bleeding
  • Arterial: bright red, spurting blood
  • Venous: dark red, steady flow
  • Capillary: slow, oozing
🩹 Steps to Stop Bleeding
  1. Apply firm pressure with a clean cloth
  2. Elevate the injured area if possible
  3. Use a bandage to keep pressure
  4. If bleeding continues, apply a tourniquet above the wound
⚠ When to Seek Medical Help
  • Bleeding won’t stop after 10 minutes
  • Blood is spurting from wound
  • Large or deep wounds
