🚨 Choking & Heimlich Maneuver

Choking occurs when an object blocks the airway, making it difficult or impossible to breathe. The Heimlich maneuver can help remove the blockage.

📍 Signs of Choking
  • Clutching the throat
  • Difficulty speaking or breathing
  • Skin, lips, or nails turning blue
🧍 Heimlich Maneuver for Adults & Children
  1. Stand behind the person
  2. Wrap your arms around their waist
  3. Make a fist and place it above the navel
  4. Grasp the fist with your other hand
  5. Perform quick, upward abdominal thrusts until the object is expelled
👶 Choking in Infants
  • Lay the infant face down on your forearm
  • Give 5 firm back blows between the shoulder blades
  • If object doesn't come out, turn over and give 5 chest thrusts
