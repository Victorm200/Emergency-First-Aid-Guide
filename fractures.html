🦴 Fractures & Broken Bones

Fractures require quick care to prevent further injury and reduce pain.

📍 Signs of a Fracture
  • Swelling and bruising
  • Visible deformity
  • Severe pain and inability to move
🩹 First Aid for Fractures
  1. Immobilize the injured area
  2. Apply a splint to prevent movement
  3. Use a sling for arm injuries
  4. Apply ice to reduce swelling
⚠ Do Not
  • Attempt to straighten a bone
  • Move the person unless necessary
